Rumor Me App 2014

The game of Telephone but with pictures...then words...then pictures

Rumor me app was a cross platform app where you joined 3-6 of your friends and made up a sentence. Then you would have to draw a picture based on a sentence. Then you'd have to type out a sentence in response to an image sent to you. Alternating until everyone has gone through.

This app was originally prototyped in Apache Cordova and then ported over to jQuery Mobile and Phonegap. Using Laravel/PHP as an API/backend. The hardest challenge was identifying game states with multiple players.
